Ingredients
   1/2 cup Milk
   1/2 cup Water
   1 Teaspoon Black tea
   1/2 teaspoon Sugar
   Pinch of ginger

Directions
1. Put water in a small utensil and bring it to a boil

2. Add sugar, tea and ginger and let it simmer for some more time

3. FInally, add milk and keep it over the stove for 2 minutes.

4. Serve and enjoy it hot !
